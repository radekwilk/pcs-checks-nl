How to measure KFC front counter speed of service
  1. Click New customer button when customer joines the queue.
  2. Click on money icon when customer finishes payment. This will make chicken meal icon clickable.
  3. Click on chicken meal icon when customer receives order.
  4. Once you click chicken meal icon, it will calculate OTD* and service time and it will display it in corresponding fields. Average OTD* and average total service times will be calculated base on completed transations. Results will be displayed in fields on the top of the form.
  5. Total service time and avg servie time are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET, RED = ABOVE TARGET (TARGET = 5 min).
  6. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that customer.
  7. If icon is active, you can overwrite payment of food received time.
  8. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page).

*OTD - Order to Delivery

How to measure KFC Drive Thru speed of service
  1. Click New car button when customer completes order at speaker post or DT order window.
  2. Click on car at the DT window icon when car arrives at pick up window. This will make chicken bucket icon clickable.
  3. Click on chicken bucket icon when car leaves with food order.
  4. Once you click chicken bucket icon, it will calculate window time and service time and it will display it in corresponding fields. Average window time and average total service times will be calculated base on completed transations. Results will be displayed in fields on the top of the form.
  5. Total service time and avg servie time are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET, RED = ABOVE TARGET (TARGET = 2 min 30 seconds).
  6. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that customer/transaction.
  7. If icon is active, you can overwrite corresponding time.
  8. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page).
How to measure KFC Home Delivery service time
  1. Click New driver button when home delivery driver arrives to the store.
  2. Click on delivery bicycle icon when home delivery driver picks up the order.
  3. Once you click on delivery bicycle icon, it will calculate home delivery service time and it will display it in the field. Average service time will be calculated base on completed transations. Results will be displayed in the field on the top of the form.
  4. Total service time and avg servie time are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET, RED = ABOVE TARGET (TARGET = 5 min).
  5. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that driver/transaction.
  6. If icon is active, you can overwrite corresponding time.
  7. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page).
How to measure Burger King front counter service time
  1. Click New customer button when Guest receives receipt or cash/card back.
  2. Click on hamburger meal icon when customer recives his/her order.
  3. Once you click hamburger icon, it will calculate front counter service time and it will display it in the field. Average service time will be calculated base on completed transations. Results will be displayed in the field on the top of the form.
  4. Total service time and avg servie time are color coded:
    Green = ON TARGET, ORANGE = PART POINTS AWARDED, RED = ABOVE TARGET (TARGET = 2 min, GOES RED ABOVE 2 min 15 seconds).
  5. Click on trash icon if you want to delete that customer/transaction.
  6. If icon is active, you can overwrite corresponding time.
  7. Click on reload page button if you want to reload the page (clear all the data on the page).
