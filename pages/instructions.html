How to get Gold Standards Report

To get Gold Standard Report go to All Programs Window, click on EPCS folder (as on attached image) and then click on Gold Standard Report.

Please see attached example of Gold Standard Report.

How to get Freshness Standards Report

Freshness Standard Report can be found in All Programs Window, click on QPM folder and then on link QPM Reports.

Once you open page as on attached picture, you can select Freshness report by clicking on link on left hand side or on link on right hand side, next to all products list. If you can not see screen as on attached picture, ensure you are on Products Mgmt tag.

Once you open Freshness Standard Report, you will see data presented as a graph. To get details you will need to clik on link called "DetailedBatchData". Link can be find at the bottom of the graph.

To switch to another product , the easiest way to do it is to click on {p} symbol which you can find at the left top corner of the report.

How to get Piece Count Report

To get Piece Count Report you will have to ask member of management team to do it for you. They have to open Management System called RMF. Then they need to follow steps below:
Menu
Report Selection
In GROUP SECTION, Select Inventory
In Reports section, select "Piece Count Report".
Select date you are looking for.

Please see attached example of Piece Count Report.

How to get Daily Scorecard

To get Daily Scorecard Report you will have to ask member of management team to do it for you. They have to open Management System called RMF. Then they need to follow steps below:
Menu
Report Selection
In GROUP SECTION, Select Inventory
In Reports section, select "Daily Scorecard". This report is always at the top of the list.
Select date you are looking for.

Please see attached example of Daily Scorecard.
