How to find AMOUNT COOKED
  1. You will need Product control sheet
  2. Add all cooked product (summary of all entries)
  3. In example above, it would be:
    • 15+15+15+15+20+30+27+20+20 = 177
  4. Enter this number into web form, under 'Amount cooked' field
  5. For chciekn add all numbers as for all other products. Heads will be recalculated into pieces for you.
How to find AMOUNT SOLD
  1. You will need Piece Count Report

    Somethimes this report is called Key Items Sales Report

  2. Look for product name at the top of the report
  3. Look for the Total number at the bottom of the report. In exapmle above, you will look for:
    • 33 for Zinger
    • 180 for Chicken
  4. Enter this number into web form, under 'Amount sold' field
How to find PHYSICAL WASTE
  1. You will need Daily Scorecard Report
  2. Find product name on the left hand side of the report
  3. Look for "Waste" column in the report. In exapmle above, you will look for:
    • Chicken original (pieces)
    • Waste = 23
  4. Enter this number into web form, under 'Physical waste' field
Amount ACCOUNTED FOR
  1. This filed is to help you to understand varainces - it is calculated for you.
  2. Amount Accounted For = Product Sold + Physical waste
  3. Where the numbers are coming from:
    • Product sold is taken from Piece Count Report
    • Physical waste is taken from DailyScorecard report
How VARIANCE pieces is calculated
  1. This filed is calculated for you.
  2. This filed is to show you difference between product recorded in PCS and total product accounted for (sold + waste).
  3. Variance quantities (pieces) = (Amount Recorded in PCS) - (Amount Accounted For)
How VARIANCE % is calculated
  1. This filed is calculated for you.
  2. If this filed is greater than 10%, it indicates that PCS wasn't recorded correctly for that product.
  3. If variance is greater than 10% for TWO or more products, it's PCS abuse.
